Mozart/Twinkle Twinkle Little Star Unit

Kindergarten-5th Grade

During this unit students:

  • Introduced as a mystery song (students guess the song based on its melody)
  • Learn about Mozart, what a composer does
  • Learn about rhythm: echo clap, antiphon, track steady beat and rhythm, write rhythm on board
  • 2nd Grade: Song dotted Twinkle Twinkle melody (student work example below)
  • Experience theme and variation form by listening and labeling sections
  • 5th grade: Compose short theme and variation piece (write 4 measure theme, create 4 measure variation by changing rhythm or adding notes)

Peter and the Wolf Unit

Kindergarten-4th Grade

During this unit students:

  • Learn about musical themes through the Peter and the Wolf visual story
  • Match musical themes to instruments
  • Play rhythmic themes on classroom percussion instruments to a SpongeBob story
  • 3rd and 4th grade: Write their own short story and composed short musical themes for each character in the story

White Stripes Unit

Kindergarten-4th Grade

During this unit students:

  • Discussed types of musical ensembles
  • Read a book based on the “We are Going to be Friends” song
  • Learn about the White Stripes band and their instruments (guitar, drum set)
  • Compare/contrast performances of “We are are Going to be Friends”
  • Performed “We are Going to be Friends” on xylophone and hand drums. (Demonstrated in the video below)
